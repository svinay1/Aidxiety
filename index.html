OVERVIEW



Welcome to Aidxiety! With the help of this app, you can help reduce your anxiety levels. Aidxiety offers four ways to help achieve its mission: relaxation, fun games, scheduling, and venting each targeting different aspects of anxiety. If your anxiety is leading you to stress out, check out the relaxation tab; if you have some downtime, play the game in the play tab; and if you feel like your days are spiralling out of control, check out the schedule page. We also have a safe and private platform availabe for you to express your feelings whenever you would like to! We want it to be a place where you feel as if you have no barriers.
